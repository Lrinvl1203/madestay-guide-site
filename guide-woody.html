📺 TV Remote Control

When watching normal TVPress the power button (1). TV power + Set‑Top Box power are turned ON/OFF at the same time.
When watching Netflix / YouTubePress each dedicated app button.
When going back to normal TVPress the external input button and set to HDMI 1.

🔥 Heat & Hot Water Settings

난방과 온수를 함께 켜기Press the power button to turn on both heating and hot water.
온수만 사용하기Press the Heating / Going out button twice. The screen should show only hot water turned on.
온도 표시Left: Heating temperature / Right: Hot water temperature.

🌬️ Air Purifier & Air Conditioner

Air PurifierUse Power and Air volume buttons. Air volume options are H / M / L.
Air ConditionerUse Power, Temperature, and Air volume controls.

Dispose at the separate collection point on the 2nd basement floor.

Food waste container

Please use a dedicated container for food waste.

Extra envelopes are under the sink.

🔄 Recycling route

Go to B2Go down to the 2nd basement floor and exit to the left.
Parking lot entranceIt is to the right of the entrance to the underground parking lot.

🛍️ General garbage route

Go to 1FGo down to the 1st floor and exit to the left.
Outside the buildingExit the building and go to the left. There is a garbage dump.

🟨 Food waste route

Go to 1FGo down to the 1st floor and exit to the right. Get out of the building.
